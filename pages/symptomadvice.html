Symptom and Advice

1. Swollen breasts

To overcome this, wear a bra that fits properly to reduce discomfort. Before buying a bra, it's a good idea for you to try it on first so you can get a bra that is the right size.

2. Nausea

"Most women who experience nausea and vomiting in the first trimester will go on to have a normal and healthy pregnancy," said obstetrician Stanley M. Berry, MD, quoted from WebM, extreme nausea and vomiting can be dangerous, Bun. This can cause weight loss, dizziness, dehydration, and electrolyte imbalance.This can be overcome by eating bland foods in the morning. Then, consume protein snacks to prevent hunger which can cause nausea. Vitamin B6 and ginger products are also very helpful in reducing these symptoms.

3. Frequent urination

This urination problem can be overcome by exercising the perineal muscles, such as Kegel exercises. To learn about this, you can ask your doctor.

4. Constipation

To overcome this, mothers need to drink lots of fluids, get enough exercise, and eat foods high in fiber. Take special vitamins for pregnant women that contain iron and vitamin C. Vitamin C will increase iron absorption.

5. Fatigue

Fatigue is a common thing experienced by young pregnant women. Your body needs rest after a day of activities. Fatigue can be anticipated by taking a short nap during the day, brisk walking, and stretching. Mothers also need to consume protein-packed snacks to restore energy.
